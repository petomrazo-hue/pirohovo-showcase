Frequently asked questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know before your first visit

How does pick-up at the stand work?
Come to Prešovská 349/38B, park right in front of the stand and order at the window. Hot dumplings are ready in 5–10 minutes — straight into your hands.
Can I order ahead?
Yes. Call us at 0915 671 603 or message us on WhatsApp — we'll prepare your dumplings for a specific time. Wolt and Bolt orders go through automatically.
Do you deliver via Wolt and Bolt?
Yes. Pirohovo is available on Wolt and Bolt Food. Delivery across all of Bratislava.
What are your opening hours?
We are open Tuesday to Sunday from 11:00 to 20:00. We are closed on Mondays.
Are the dumplings handmade?
Yes. Every dumpling is shaped by hand every day. We mix the dough fresh each morning — no moulds, no machines.
Can I pay by card?
Yes. We accept contactless card payments and cash at the stand window.
Are allergens listed?
Yes. Allergens are listed for every menu item in accordance with EU Regulation No. 1169/2011. The full list is at pirohovo.eu/alergeny. If you have a severe allergy, please contact us before ordering — cross-contamination in the kitchen cannot be excluded.
Do you have seasonal specials?
Yes. The menu changes with the seasons — for example apricot dumplings in summer. Check the latest special by phone or on Instagram @pirohovo.
Still have a question?
Message us on WhatsApp — we'll reply within 5 minutes.
